About this Question:

This is an example of a question which is required. This means that unless you have answered the question, you will not be able to proceed.

Notice that as you would expect, the "Next" button does not show unless you have answered the question or previously completed this question. However, once you pick an answer, it will save, lock the option controls, and allow you to continue.

The first time you answer, the the quiz system hides the question & options and then shows you the appropriate response. On subsequent visits to this page however (via the back button or during a Review) the quiz system will show the question, options (which will be locked) with your choice selected, and the response to your chosen answer.
